c++ stl contain major 4 parts 
1.container (contains basic data str.)
2.iterator 
3.algoritham
4.functors

container-: sequential (vector,stack,queue,pair(NA))
            ordered (map,set, multimap,multiset)
            unordered(unordered_map,unordered_set)
  
nested container -: vector<vector<int>>
                    map<int,vector<int>>
                    set<pair<int,string>>
                    vector<map<int,set<int>>>



iterator -: points to memory address of container 
           begin(),end()
           vector<int>::iterator it 



algorithams -:upper_bound , lower_bound , sort(comparator) , max_element , min_element ,
             accumulate , reverse , count , find , next permutation , prev permutation
            


functors-: classes which act as funtions  





precomputation technique-: results are pre computed and stored for the further
                           computation
